Tasty Onion Chicken

dairy butter 0.5 cup,sauce worcestershire-sauce 1 tablespoon,seasoning ground-mustard 1 teaspoon,ingredient french-fried-onions 2.8 ounces,chicken four-boneless-skinless-chicken-breast-halves 16 ounces
In a shallow bowl, combine butter, Worcestershire sauce and mustard. Place onions in another shallow bowl. Dip chicken in butter mixture, then coat with onions.
Place in a greased 11x7-in. baking dish; drizzle with remaining butter mixture. Bake, uncovered, at 400 degrees for 20-25 minutes or until a thermometer reads 165 degrees.
