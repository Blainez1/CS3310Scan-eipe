Peppered Pork with Mushroom Sauce

mushroom
30 min
4
208
Lunch,Dinner
oil olive-oil 2 tablespoons,pork peppercorn-pork-tenderloin-cut-into-3/4-inch-slices 1 pound,vegetable mushrooms 0.5 cup,vegetable chopped-onion 0.25 cup,flour all-purpose-flour 2 tablespoons,broth reduced-sodium-beef-broth 1 cup
In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil over medium heat. Brown pork on both sides. Remove from pan.
In same pan, heat remaining oil over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms and onion; cook and stir until tender, 4-5 minutes.
In a small bowl, mix flour and broth until smooth. Stir into mushroom mixture. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until sauce is thickened. Return pork to pan. Cook until a thermometer inserted in pork reads 145 degrees.
